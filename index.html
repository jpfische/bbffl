Month Out Memorandum

Year Nine boys and girls, absolutely amazing stuff. For most of you all to put up with this tomfoolery for nine years is amazing. I get sick of myself pretty quickly, so let’s give a round of applause to all of you. Let’s also give a round of applause to Nohalty. Our Two Time (I know right? Had to really think if that happened or not) Throphyless Champion. Jared, if you don’t bring that fucking trophy with you this year I swear on all things holy I beat fill up a pillowcase with bars of soap and beat the living shit out of you. But don’t worry sweet prince, you may be giving up one trophy, but guess what? Aaron can finally cleanse his house of that Holy Holy Debacle of Timothy Tebow. Fun Fact, he made a AA All-Star Game this year. His stat line is reminiscent of Justin during our fateful softball season, so it was really a publicity stunt.

Now, let’s talk about this year. I don’t know about you guys, but I’ve got a feeling. A feeling deep down in my plums. This is my year. I know I say that every year, and will continue to say that every single year, but this year is different. Went down to the farmer’s market, picked me up some juicy, succulent plums. Three for a dollar in fact, heck of a deal. Flirted with the nice farm girl, hell of a Saturday. Last year a came close. Actually scratch that, I came earlier. I blew my load prematurely. As the late, great, Alan Krebs once said. The key is stamina, you don’t want to come shooting out of the gates too early, you need a nice tempo and be ready to go at just the right moment. I’ve learned from my record setting regular season and will be one a missionary to bring it home this year. Alan Krebs has never steered me wrong, ain’t that right Nohalty? And if you guys have no idea what the hell that was, a delightful YouTube clip is below to fill in the gaps.



Draft Talk!!!!! We will be drafting in the sacred hollows of Bloomington, IN this year. Home to some of the greatest triumps of our time. The time Frank and Staten cut at least 15 years off their lives living next to KFC. The time Matt fell down a hole. The time Jared passed out at 7:15 AM at a tailgate. The time Michael came to Frank’s defense in the only heroic action we twelve have every participated in. The time Bozell defeated me close to 12 consecutive beer pong games. And finally the time we were crowned 4th Annual Beer Olympic Champions. What time will this year be? Can we add any achievement to that harrowing list? Doubtful, but we will try. Rough Outline Below of what draft weekend will look like.

5 PM Friday, August 24th.

Fish and anyone else from the down south will depart. I have room for just one person as I’m hauling a 50 inch monitor for our sure to be flawless Fan Draft Experience. We have two rooms for Friday and Saturday. Let me know early if you plan on going or not going. That way I can cut the room down on Friday to save us all some cash if we only have 4-5 Friday night

9 AM Saturday, August 25th

We will be teeing off at the prestigious Cascades Country Club of Bloomington. Tee time yet to be decided, but I’m angling for the 9ish range. So you Saturday only guys, make sure to set those alarms and be on the road by 7.

3 PM Saturday, August 25th

Draft will commence. As stated earlier, FAN DRAFT EXPERIENCE. As of this Michael is the only one not going to be live, so we are going to reserve the online draft server for him only. That was the flaw last year. We were pretty much trying to shit a bowling ball through a drinking straw last year with their servers. I have confirmed with the developers that 1-2 on and it should be smooth.

Of course, I’ll be running a live Google Doc as well like last year when it eventually fails. Because, you know, that’s what happens when we try and do anything fun.
